WELCOME TO STARS!

Your gateway to real-time Earth & space satellite images.

Here at STARS, we've built a global network of ground stations that tune into analog APT signals from orbiting NOAA satellites. Each transmission is decoded, stitched, and transformed into crisp, real-time imagery of our planet and beyond. Whether you're just browsing the public feed or syncing your own receiver, STARS makes satellite photography accessible to everyone.

Sign up or log in to access your personal dashboard, upload your own captures, and customize your ground-station settings.
